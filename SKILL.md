# TopoLift Negotiation Atoms — Paused

**This skill bundle is paused while our offering matures.**

The TopoLift Negotiation Atoms are temporarily off the downloadable-skill
channel. The reasoning capability — and significantly more — is currently
available through the **TopoLift Reasoning API**. A lite distribution may
return in a future form once the offering settles.

## Why the change

Agentic negotiation is moving fast. To keep the underlying topological
reasoning coherent across agents, frameworks, and use cases, we are serving
it as a managed reasoning service while the product matures. Pausing the
downloadable bundle lets us iterate on the atoms themselves without
fragmenting older copies in the wild.

## Where to go now

**API access:** [topolift.ai](https://topolift.ai)
**Contact:** atoms@topolift.ai

If you're building agent-to-agent negotiation, multi-agent coordination,
compassion-aware dialogue, or alignment-sensitive bargaining workflows, reach
out — we are actively looking for serious design partners.

## A note for existing users

Versions of this bundle distributed before this pause remain under their
original Apache-2.0 license. Updates on this channel are paused for now;
follow [topolift.ai](https://topolift.ai) for news on when (and how) a lite
distribution returns.

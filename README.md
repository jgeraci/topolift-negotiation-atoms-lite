# TopoLift Negotiation Atoms (Lite) — Paused

**This bundle is paused while our offering matures.** The repository is
archived for now; a lite distribution may return in a future form.

The TopoLift Negotiation Atoms reasoning capability is currently available
through the **TopoLift Reasoning API** at [topolift.ai](https://topolift.ai).

## Why

Agentic negotiation is moving fast. To keep the underlying topological
reasoning coherent across agents, frameworks, and use cases, we are serving
it as a managed reasoning service while the product matures. Pausing the
downloadable bundle lets us iterate on the atoms themselves without
fragmenting older copies in the wild.

## Where to go now

- **API:** [topolift.ai](https://topolift.ai)
- **Contact:** atoms@topolift.ai

If you are building agent-to-agent negotiation, multi-agent coordination,
compassion-aware dialogue, or alignment-sensitive bargaining workflows, get in
